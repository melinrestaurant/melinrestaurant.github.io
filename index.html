Our Story

Welcome

Established in 1968, Melin is a traditional family restaurant, renowned for Vietnamese and Chinese cuisine. Melin has been presented with numerous culinary and customer service awards, including the title of "Best Restaurant in the Redlands" from the local council.

With over 35 years experience, Executive Chef Kim Xi Tran is wholeheartedly committed to cooking nutritious, delicious food. Award-winning host, Monica Tran, ensures everyone feels welcome with her heartfelt hospitality.

At Melin, every dish is freshly cooked with top-quality produce. All ingredients are sourced from local Australian farmers and suppliers. The restaurant caters for the dietary requirements of all customers. Melin is proud to provide excellent food, service and value.
